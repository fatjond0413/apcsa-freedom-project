# Presentation Plan

## Hook
* For my hook, I plan to ask to the audience if they know a platformer game where they play as a blue guy. If they say yes, then I will introduce my game as say "Well here is another one". If not then I will introduce my game and say "Well you are going to know one by the end of this presentation.

## Product
* I will show my product through a video of me playing my project

## Process

* I first plan to show links to the youtube videos that helped me understand Godot and how I learned it.
* Then I plan to show three snippits of my code to show MVP and beyond MVP
    * For my first snippit I plan to show the code for my chracter motion so the audience can see what makes that layer move
    * For my second snippit I plan to break it up to two slides to show how I make the blocks for the map and how I place them/make the player stand on them.
    * For my third snippit I plan to show is the code for the moving tile so I can show the audience how I made the tile move

## Conclusion
* Challenges
  * For my takeaways I plan to list my biggest challenges and how they were challenging to me during the making of my product.
* Takeaways
  * I plan to list the lessons I have learned from my challenges and possible solutions to these challenges.
